# Research — Agent Rules

## Context
Self-directed study on ML, RAG pipelines, and adjacent topics. Not tied to coursework or internship — this is deep personal learning.

## Three action tiers (read before suggesting work)
- **Learning** — reading, summarizing, note-taking on a topic
- **Basic execution** — small tests or toy projects to apply what was learned; lives inside the relevant subfolder here
- **Deep execution** — full-scale projects; these live in `projects/`, not here

When the user asks what to do next, respect which tier they're in. Don't jump to deep execution unless they ask.

## Behavior in this folder

**Citations matter:** When referencing papers, models, or benchmarks, include the source (arxiv ID, author, year). Don't state capabilities as fact without a citation.

**No hallucinated benchmarks:** If you don't know a model's exact score on a benchmark, say so. Don't interpolate.

**Subfolders:** `RAG-pipelines/`, `ML/`, `misc/`. Each may have its own `_context_*.md` — read it before adding notes to that subfolder.

**Note format:** Follow vault frontmatter schema. For research notes, `type/reference` or `type/lab` is typical. Tag with the subfolder name (e.g. `course/rag` or area tag as appropriate).

**Don't create files** unless asked.
